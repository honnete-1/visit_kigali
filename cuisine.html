Rwandan Cuisine

Taste Kigali through isombe, brochettes, local vegetables, fresh market produce, and rich Rwandan coffee.

Must-try foods

  • Isombe: cassava leaves cooked into a rich green stew, often served with rice, beans, or ugali.
  • Brochettes: grilled skewers served in many restaurants and evening spots.
  • Ugali / Ubugali: a filling staple that pairs well with vegetables, beans, and sauce.
  • Ibirayi: potatoes often served with sauce, vegetables, or meat.
  • Rwandan coffee: a strong part of the country's hospitality and export identity.

Kimironko Market & food culture

Kimironko Market is useful for seeing Kigali's food culture, fresh produce, fabrics, and everyday trade. Visitors should move respectfully, keep belongings safe, and ask vendors before taking close-up photos.
