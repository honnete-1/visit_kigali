Kigali Convention Centre

Africa's most iconic venue — where world-class events meet breathtaking design.

An Architectural Marvel

The Kigali Convention Centre (KCC) is one of Africa's most striking buildings, rising elegantly from Kigali's skyline like a modern crown. Officially opened in 2016, the KCC was designed by the acclaimed Lebanese firm Khatib & Alami and has become a defining symbol of Rwanda's extraordinary transformation.

The building's most iconic feature is its massive glass dome — the second largest of its kind in Africa — which illuminates the night sky in a breathtaking rainbow of colours. Standing beneath it, you can't help but feel you're witnessing Rwanda's future in real time.

The KCC hosts everything from international summits and African Union meetings to music concerts, expos, and private galas. It is also home to the luxurious Marriott Hotel, making it a complete destination in itself.

Why Visit the KCC?

🌟

World-Class Events

The KCC hosts over 200 events per year, from G7 satellite meetings to the prestigious African Philanthropy Forum.

🏨

Marriott Hotel

Directly connected to the KCC is Kigali Marriott Hotel — 254 rooms of luxury with rooftop pool and spa.

🍽️

Fine Dining

Multiple restaurants and bars on site, including the legendary Shokola Lite and the Ejo Restaurant with city views.

📸

Photo Hotspot

The illuminated dome at sunset is Kigali's most photographed sight. The viewing area on the hill is perfect for shots!

🎵

Entertainment Hub

International artists, from Afrobeats stars to classical concerts, regularly perform in the KCC's main auditorium.

🌿

Green & Sustainable

The KCC was designed with sustainability in mind — solar panels, rainwater harvesting, and smart energy systems throughout.

Getting to the KCC

🚕

Moto-Taxi

The fastest and most fun way around Kigali! Jump on a moto (motorcycle taxi) from anywhere in the city. Just say "KCC" and the driver will know. Costs approximately RWF 1,000–3,000.

🚌

City Bus

Kigali's modern Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) system has a stop near the KCC. Line 101 from Nyabugogo Bus Park goes directly past the Convention Centre.

🚗

Car / Taxi

Use the Yegopay or SafeMotos app for safe, metered taxis. The KCC has ample parking on-site. GPS Coordinates: -1.9469° S, 30.0587° E.

🚶

Walking

If you're staying in the Kiyovu or Kimihurura areas, the KCC is within a pleasant 15–25 minute walk through Kigali's famously clean and safe streets.
