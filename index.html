Steps to Plan Your
Kigali Visit

Planning a trip to Rwanda is easier than you think. Follow these steps and you'll be sipping Rwandan coffee on a Kigali hilltop in no time!

  1. 01
    Check Visa Requirements

    Most nationalities can get a 30-day visa on arrival. Check the Rwanda Migration Authority website for your country.

  2. 02
    Book Your Flights to RWF Kigali Airport

    Kigali International Airport (KGL) is well-connected via Ethiopian Airlines, RwandAir, and Brussels Airlines.

  3. 03
    Choose Your Accommodation

    From the luxury Kigali Marriott to budget-friendly guesthouses in Kimihurura — there's a bed for every budget.

  4. 04
    Plan Your Itinerary

    Mix cultural visits (Memorial, museums) with nature (Nyungwe Forest, Volcanoes NP) and city life (Nyamirambo, KCC).

  5. 05
    Get Travel Insurance

    Always a good idea! Rwanda is very safe but insurance covers medical emergencies and flight delays.

  6. 06
    Pack Smart — Rwanda Has Eco Rules!

    Rwanda bans plastic bags, so bring reusable bags. Dress modestly when visiting religious or memorial sites.
