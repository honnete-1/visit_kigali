Steps to Plan Your Kigali Visit

Planning a trip to Rwanda is easier when you prepare the basics early.

01

Check visa requirements

Most visitors can check official Rwanda migration guidance before travelling.

02

Book your flights to Kigali

Kigali International Airport connects visitors to the city and nearby attractions.

03

Choose accommodation

Pick a place near your activities, such as Kimihurura, Kiyovu, Remera, or the KCC area.

04

Plan your itinerary

Mix cultural visits, food experiences, history, and city-night views.

05

Pack smart

Bring comfortable shoes, reusable bags, and respectful clothing for memorial or religious sites.

Kigali is known for clean streets, green hills, and a calm city atmosphere.
